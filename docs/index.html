Quickstart

From search to saved opportunities.

Ebisu turns a plain role and location into ATS-source Google results, then helps you save the roles worth revisiting.

Step 1
1

Search from the popup

Enter the job description, enter the location, and click Go to launch a search across supported ATS sources.

Step 2
2

Review ATS-source results

Google opens the direct Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, and Workday results. Ebisu adds status controls inline so you can mark roles as seen, saved, or applied without losing momentum.

Step 3
3

Track everything in History

Saved roles stay inside Ebisu so you can reopen promising leads, clean up stale listings, and keep your search organized as you apply.

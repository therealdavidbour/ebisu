Local-first by design

Your job history stays in your browser.

Ebisu stores saved job records in Chrome local storage. It does not require an account, does not sell user data, and does not transmit your job history to an external server.

Chrome permissions

Only what the extension needs.

Storage

Saves job history, status, timestamps, and optional listing dates locally in Chrome storage.

Tabs

Opens generated Google searches and saved job links from the extension popup.

Google Search

Identifies supported ATS links in search results and displays Ebisu status controls.

Supported ATS sites

Shows the Ebisu toolbar on Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, and Workday job pages.
